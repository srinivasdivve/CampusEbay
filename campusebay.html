Introduction

The world we are living in today guarantees us the products with the best quality and maximum life time. But we do not need the products all the time throughout the life. We simply throw away the product. It has serious effects such as when some other customer needs the products he will purchase that again leading to the increased pollution and wastage of many resources. Further a used product costs less than that of the original product. So some customers who are unable to purchase the new product can purchase the used product. In order to do this we need a system or a source which provides a platform to buy or sell these products.
